About Global Placers

Global Placers is an Africa-based Recruitment and Training company built to solve one of the most pressing challenges businesses face today employee deficiency. We partner with companies of all sizes to deliver fast, efficient, and strategic hiring solutions across a wide range of industries. With deep market understanding, innovative sourcing strategies, and a strong global network, we're able to find the right talent anyone, anywhere in the world. Our team brings a blend of local insight and international reach, allowing us to support hiring needs across Africa, North America, Europe, Asia, LATAM, and Australia. From technical, engineering roles to executive leadership, we match companies with professionals who drive real impact. At Global Placers, we don't just fill roles we close talent gaps with precision and purpose
